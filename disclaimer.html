Non-Clinical Services

The Mr. Metaphysical Coaching & Personal Growth platform ("Platform"), operated by Mr. Metaphysical Health and Wellness, Inc., provides personal development coaching services and facilitates connections between individuals and independent coaches. All services offered through this Platform are non-clinical.

Specifically, the services offered through this Platform:

  • Are not psychotherapy, counseling, or mental health treatment;
  • Do not diagnose, treat, prevent, or cure any mental health condition, psychological disorder, or medical condition;
  • Are not subject to state or federal mental health licensing or regulatory requirements;
  • Are not covered by or filed with health insurance;
  • Do not establish a clinical, therapeutic, or confidential professional relationship.

Coaches Are Independent Practitioners

Coaches listed in the Platform directory are independent practitioners. They are not employees, agents, or representatives of Mr. Metaphysical Health and Wellness, Inc. The Company does not supervise, direct, or control their coaching methods, communications, or client relationships.

The Company reviews coaches for general values alignment but does not independently verify credentials, certifications, or professional backgrounds claimed by coaches. Clients are responsible for conducting their own due diligence when selecting a coach.

Not a Substitute for Therapy

Personal growth coaching is a distinct service from psychotherapy. If you are experiencing symptoms of depression, anxiety, post-traumatic stress, or any other mental health condition, or if you have a history of psychiatric illness, please consult a licensed mental health professional before beginning a coaching engagement.

Coaching is appropriate for people who are functioning well and seeking to grow, develop, or navigate a life challenge. It is not appropriate as a primary intervention for active clinical mental health conditions.

For clinical mental health services, please visit our affiliated clinical practice at mrmetaphysical.io.

No Guarantee of Outcomes

Personal growth coaching is a process, not a product. Outcomes vary significantly based on individual circumstances, the quality of the coaching relationship, the client's level of engagement and follow-through, and numerous other factors outside the control of the coach or the Platform.

Neither Mr. Metaphysical Health and Wellness, Inc. nor any coach listed in the directory makes any guarantee, express or implied, regarding specific outcomes, results, or changes that may or may not result from a coaching engagement. Any testimonials or examples described on this Platform represent individual experiences and are not guarantees of future results.
