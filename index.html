The Process

Simple. Focused. Effective.

1

Browse the Directory

Find a coach whose approach, specialty, and style resonate with you. Read their bio, review their focus areas, and see if the fit feels right.

2

Book a Discovery Call

Most coaches offer a free 20–30 minute call to explore fit, clarify your goals, and see whether working together makes sense.

3

Begin the Work

Coaching typically runs in 4–12 week engagements, meeting weekly or biweekly. Between-session accountability is part of the structure.

4

Track Your Growth

Real change is measurable. Your coach helps you define clear milestones and markers so you can see — not just feel — the difference.
