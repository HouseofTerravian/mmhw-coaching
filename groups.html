About the Groups

Growth Doesn't Have to Be Solitary.

Our personal development groups bring together people working on similar challenges in a structured, facilitated environment. Each group meets weekly, runs in 8–12 week cohorts, and is designed to create real movement — not just conversation. You'll leave each session with something to do, not just something to think about.

Groups are led by coaches and facilitators from our network. Enrollment is intentionally limited to ensure genuine connection and focused work. These are not passive webinars or lecture series — they are active, participatory containers built around your growth.

The Value

Why Groups Work

There is something that happens in a well-facilitated group that doesn't happen in one-on-one work. You hear yourself in other people. You get challenged from directions you didn't expect. You realize the thing you thought was a personal failure is actually a shared human experience — and that changes your relationship to it.

Our groups are not support groups in the traditional sense. They are development containers with structure, accountability, and direction. You're not just sharing — you're working.

The limitation on group size is intentional. Twelve people is the upper limit for genuine connection and focused facilitation. Most groups run smaller.

👥

Curated Enrollment

Every participant is reviewed before acceptance to ensure the group composition supports real development.

📝

Structured Format

Each session has a clear arc: check-in, core content, application, and accountability. You leave every session with a clear next action.

🌟

Peer Accountability

Between sessions, participants stay connected for check-ins and accountability. The group doesn't stop when the session ends.
