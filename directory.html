How It Works

From Browse to Booked

Finding the right coach is simpler than it sounds. Here's what to expect.

1

Filter & Browse

Use the filter buttons to narrow by specialty. Read bios carefully — the coach's language and approach tell you a lot about fit before you even talk.

2

Book a Discovery Call

Click "Book Discovery Call" to reach out. Discovery calls are free, 20–30 minutes, and no-pressure. You're exploring whether working together makes sense.

3

Discuss Your Goals

Come with a rough sense of what you want to work on. Your coach will help clarify it — but you need to bring the honesty. The better your self-disclosure, the better the fit assessment.

4

Start Your Engagement

If the fit is right, you'll agree on an engagement format, cadence, and investment. Most coaches can begin within 1–2 weeks of an initial call.
